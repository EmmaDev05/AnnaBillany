I'm Anna, a multi-disciplinary visual artist from Sunderland who is now residing in Stockport and Manchester.
My art, which draws inspiration from my love of fantasy and early computer graphics, can be classified as cyber-surreal.
I frequently combine 2D and 3D methods of creation and push myself to see how various mediums might collaborate.
